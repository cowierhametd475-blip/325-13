About FRKLME

FRKLME is a registered financial information consulting enterprise headquartered in Southampton, England. Founded in 2015 by a team of senior financial analysts and risk management specialists from leading global financial institutions (including Barclays, HSBC and JP Morgan), we focus on providing transparent, objective financial information services to clients worldwide.

Our team of 45+ professionals includes CFA charterholders, FRM certified risk managers, quantitative analysts and industry specialists with deep expertise in sectors ranging from technology and healthcare to energy and real estate. This diverse skill set allows us to provide holistic financial guidance across multiple industries and asset classes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to break information asymmetry in the financial industry. We utilize multi-source market data, professional macroeconomic research and one-on-one consultant support to help every client make rational financial decisions. We believe that access to high-quality financial analysis should not be limited to large institutions or high-net-worth individuals – it should be available to anyone seeking to improve their financial outcomes.

Regulatory Compliance

We strictly comply with UK financial service industry standards, regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) under registration number FRKL58729. We maintain independent third-party analysis positioning, and never participate in asset fund management or proprietary trading to guarantee absolute objectivity of our consulting reports.
